### 🔬 Research & Professional Focus

#### **Electrical Engineering & Power Systems**
I specialize in the analysis of active distribution networks and microgrids. My primary research focus is **High Impedance Fault (HIF) Detection** and **Topology Identification**. I develop algorithms to detect non-linear arcing faults where fault current magnitudes are comparable to load currents, ensuring grid safety and reliability.

#### **AI & Data Science**
My approach to engineering problems is heavily data-driven. I implement **Artificial Intelligence** and **Statistical Learning** frameworks for feature extraction and pattern recognition in power systems. This includes the application of signal processing and machine learning to distinguish between transient loads and actual fault conditions, referencing modern methodologies (e.g., Taheri 2019, Zhang 2024).

#### **Software Engineering**
I bridge the gap between theoretical research and robust implementation. My software background ranges from **System-Level Programming** (C/C++) for high-performance simulations to **Full-Stack Development** (TypeScript/JavaScript) for technical dashboards. I maintain a professional DevOps workflow utilizing Linux environments, Git, and automated documentation.
