### 🔬 Research Focus
My research focuses on the intersection of **Signal Processing** and **Power Distribution Systems**, with a specific emphasis on:
* **High Impedance Fault (HIF) Detection:** Developing algorithms to identify low-current, arcing faults that bypass conventional overcurrent protection.
* **Topology Identification:** Leveraging voltage and current measurements for real-time grid mapping in active distribution networks and microgrids.
* **Microgrid Stability:** Analysis of data-driven methods for maintaining reliability in systems with high penetration of Distributed Generation (DG).

> **Current Literature Focus:** Implementing frameworks based on Taheri (2019) and Zhang (2024) for robust fault feature extraction.
